spicaka-studio.

spicaka studio creates UX for tech-driven Art and Design

spicaka-studio in Berlin is run by irina spicaka, who after more than 8 years of experience working in the design, art and tech industry, built the studio as a space for creatives and technologists to work on exciting and innovative projects.

we use Conceptual & Design Thinking as a base.

we apply field studies, user-centered approach, AI, cognitive science, human-machine interaction principles to research, define, ideate, prototype, build solutions for UI, VR, AR, immersive interactive artworks.
